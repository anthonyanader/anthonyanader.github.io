Story

I've always been drawn to music regardless of the professional path chosen. It wasn't just listening or identifying to a particular genre; I was captivated by sound. The layers, textures, and dynamics of individual instruments and musical elements combined to form a song.

The best way to describe my creative process stems from writing melodies, composing, and sequencing complete tracks to designing and implementing algorithms and data structure into my projects.

I was able to bridge several areas such as music, business, and engineering to drive an idea, turning it into a project or product, and eventually monetize it.
