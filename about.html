Story

I've always been drawn to music regardless of the professional path chosen. It wasn't just listening or identifying to a particular genre; I was captivated by sound. The layers, textures, and dynamics of musical elements that come together to form a song.

I particularly enjoyed the creative process: composing melodies, sampling, and shaping sounds followed by sequencing, automating and arranging elements to create a track.

I get often asked why I pursued a business degree and quickly start another engineering degree.

I particularly enjoyed the creative process of sampling sounds, to shaping them to fit particular place in the sound spectrum, sequencing and arranging the individual pieces and mastering the whole track.
